About Weight Beef

We're a small, passion-driven hobby business raising high-quality beef with care right here in the mountains of Utah. Our cattle carry the trusted 'W8' brand, reflecting our commitment to sustainable practices, animal welfare, and getting premium, grass-fed and grain-finished cuts directly to your table—affordable and without the middleman markup.

Why Beef Prices Are Up

We've tracked prices at the Salina, Utah Livestock Auction for over 5 years. Live cattle costs have risen steadily due to higher feed prices, demand, and supply factors. This helps explain our one modest price adjustment since starting in 2020—we're still offering direct-from-ranch value while market rates climb.
