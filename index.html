About Weight Beef

We're a small, passion-driven hobby business raising high-quality beef with care right here in the mountains of Utah. Our cattle carry the trusted 'W8' brand, reflecting our commitment to sustainable practices, animal welfare, and getting premium, grass-fed and grain-finished cuts directly to your table—affordable and without the middleman markup.
