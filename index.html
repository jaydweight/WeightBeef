Why Beef Prices Are Up

We've tracked prices at the Salina, Utah Livestock Auction for over 5 years. Live cattle costs have risen steadily due to higher feed prices, demand, and supply factors. This helps explain our one modest price adjustment since starting in 2020—we're still offering direct-from-ranch value while market rates climb.
